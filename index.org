Relic is a language designed for the at-home hobby programmer. It has the
following design goals:
1. Provide a powerful tool which enables rapid development and
   iteration. Further, it should be possible to easily develop domain-specific
   tools to assist an individual programmer.
2. Enable a broad range of styles, to suit the taste of the individual
   programmer. This includes powerful introspection and metaprogramming
   facilities.
3. Encompass a broad range of potential performance vs abstraction
   profiles. Relic should enable all kinds of projects, such as embedded
   development, performant programs such as games, or highly abstract programs
   with complex type constraints or even dynamic types.
